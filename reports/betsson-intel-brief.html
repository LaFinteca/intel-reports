01

What is Betsson Group?

Betsson Group is one of Europe's largest publicly listed online gambling companies, trading on Nasdaq Stockholm Large Cap under the ticker BETS-B. Founded in 1963 as a land-based gaming company, it has grown into a multinational operator running sports betting, online casino, live casino, and poker products across 23 licensed jurisdictions.

The group operates a portfolio of brands — Betsson, Betsafe, Rizk, Kaboo, BetFirst, and Europebet — all powered by a single proprietary technology platform called Techsson. Techsson handles everything: player account management, game aggregation, bonuses, compliance tools, and critically, payment routing. One platform means one central payment integration point for the entire group.

In 2025, Betsson generated €1.197 billion in revenue. LatAm alone contributed €84.7M in Q2 2025 alone (+35.4% year-on-year) — a record quarter — making it the group's fastest-growing region. The company holds EUR 341.8M in cash (Q1 2026) and funds its growth from operating cash flow. This is a tier-1 iGaming operator where a PSP relationship, once established, is worth millions annually.

How does the money work?

Betsson's payment infrastructure is split by market. There is no single global PSP — each jurisdiction runs its own payment stack, all connected into the Techsson PAM platform. Techsson acts as the central router: it receives a deposit or withdrawal instruction, identifies the player's market and currency, and dispatches the request to the relevant local PSP integration.

This architecture is both a strength and a vulnerability. A PSP failure in one integration propagates instantly across all brands in that market — because all brands share the same Techsson core.

Peru — The group's most important LatAm market

Peru is where Betsson has made its biggest LatAm bet. Three separate brands operate simultaneously — betsson.pe (1.9M monthly visits), inkabet.pe (1.3M), and betsafe.com/pe (39,924 from Peru). Combined: over 3.2M monthly visits. One PSP integration failure affects three brands and over 3 million monthly sessions. Inkabet has a separate operational structure — potentially a separate payment integration, meaning LaFinteca could enter via Inkabet without navigating the full group Techsson integration.

Brazil — The infrastructure-building phase

The Brazil regulatory framework is the strictest in LatAm: Pix is the only permitted deposit/withdrawal method (Law 14.790/2023). Credit cards, crypto, and independent e-wallets are banned. Betsson's current Pix processor appears to be Nexta Payment Services Ltd (partially confirmed). Reclame Aqui score of 3.2–4.2/10 with only 34.3% resolution rate suggests payment friction is contributing to poor user experience. betsson.com.br has no SimilarWeb data yet — it is in soft launch and PSP conversations are happening right now.

Colombia — The crown jewel

betsson.co generates 2.04M monthly visits, making it the dominant iGaming domain in Colombia (bet365 generates only 67,310 Colombian visits; Betano generates 46,637). Betsson is the clear market leader. The new 19% VAT on gambling transactions adds margin pressure — they need better payment infrastructure to protect margins, not fewer PSP options.

Tier 1 — Immediate: Brazil Pix infrastructure (single-PSP concentration risk, soft launch phase, infrastructure being built right now) and Peru payment parity across three brands. Tier 2 — Medium-term: Colombia stack optimization, Argentina infrastructure buildout, Mexico SPEI/OXXO optimization.
