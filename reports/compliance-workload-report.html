What "needs attention" actually measures

It's based on Jira's Updated timestamp — the last time anything changed on the task itself: a status move, a comment, a field edit, an assignee change, an attachment. If that's 30+ days old, the task is flagged — unless one of its subtasks has been updated more recently, in which case the parent is counted as active too. It also can't see activity outside Jira — a phone call or an email to the counter-party won't move this number unless someone logs it on the task.
